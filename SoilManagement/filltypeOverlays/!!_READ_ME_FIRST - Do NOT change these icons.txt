
If you want your map to use different icons for these spray-types,
then please DO NOT modify them in SoilManagement.ZIP !

Instead, place the icon-files in your own map-mod, in a folder called; fruitHuds

Example:

    ...\MODS\<yourMapMod>\fruitHuds\

        hud_spray_kalk.dds
        hud_spray_kalk_small.dds

        hud_spray_fertilizer.dds
        hud_spray_fertilizer_small.dds
        hud_spray_fertilizer2.dds
        hud_spray_fertilizer2_small.dds
        hud_spray_fertilizer3.dds
        hud_spray_fertilizer3_small.dds

        hud_spray_herbicide.dds
        hud_spray_herbicide_small.dds
        hud_spray_herbicide2.dds
        hud_spray_herbicide2_small.dds
        hud_spray_herbicide3.dds
        hud_spray_herbicide3_small.dds
